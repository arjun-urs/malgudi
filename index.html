About Malgudi Spice

Welcome to Malgudi Spice, where we bring the authentic flavors of Karnataka and South India to your table. Our restaurant is named after the fictional town created by R.K. Narayan, embodying the warmth, simplicity, and rich cultural heritage of South India.


At Malgudi Spice, we specialize in traditional Karnataka and South Indian cuisine, prepared with love using time-honored recipes. Every dish is crafted from organic ingredients, made fresh daily without any preservatives or artificial additives. We believe in the purity of homemade cooking that brings comfort and nostalgia with every bite.


From crispy dosas to aromatic bisibele bath, from tangy rasam to sweet holige, our menu celebrates the diverse culinary traditions of Karnataka. Each meal is a journey through the flavors that truly feel like home.
